What We Build

The full stack, handled.

We don't do strategy decks and leave. We build, run, and own execution until the numbers are where they need to be.

01
Content Engine

Your TOF game is often already working. We build MOF and BOF content into the mix so viral reach actually converts.

  • MOF educational reels — frameworks, transformations, breakdowns
  • BOF result reels, income proof, direct CTA content
  • Carousels engineered for saves and profile authority
  • Every caption gets engineered CTA copy with DM keyword triggers
02
Funnel Infrastructure

A purpose-built landing page with a free VSL, gated behind an opt-in. High-ticket applicants route to book-a-call. Mid-budget to a sales page. Lower intent into email nurture.

  • Landing page with VSL or training opt-in
  • Typeform qualification — budget, timeline, intent
  • Split routing: call flow, sales page, or nurture
  • Full checkout and payment infrastructure
03
DM & Nurture System

An AI setter goes live in your DMs immediately to catch every inbound from existing traffic. No more leads going cold while you sleep.

  • 24/7 AI setter qualifying inbound DMs
  • Telegram broadcast channel built and seeded
  • Daily story system scripted to push followers to DMs
  • Email welcome sequence live on day one
04
Offer Stack

Three tiers built to capture every level of audience readiness — from viral traffic to serious buyers ready to invest $5–10k.

  • Low-ticket entry product at $97–$197 for viral traffic
  • Mid-ticket group cohort or course at $1,500–$2,500
  • High-ticket 1-on-1 or agency offer at $5,000–$10,000
  • Offer positioning, pricing strategy, and sales copy
05
Launch Sequence

Multi-week waitlist campaign. Daily stories, Typeform opt-in, Telegram broadcast, launch emails. The fastest path to a $100k+ launch.

  • Full launch calendar from waitlist to close
  • VSL, application funnel, 48-hour urgency close
  • Pre-launch email warming sequence
  • Challenge funnels and multi-episode pitch frameworks
06
Sales System

AI setter handles all inbound 24/7. Pre-recorded voice notes deploy post-qualification. Human setters follow up. No unqualified call ever gets booked.

  • AI setter routes and qualifies all DM leads
  • Voice note sequences to build trust pre-call
  • Human setter follow-up on warm leads
  • Closer playbook for all high-ticket calls
