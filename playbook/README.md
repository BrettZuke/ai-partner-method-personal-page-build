# Operator Playbook

> Operator workflow SOPs for AI Partner Method students. Use these **outside** the website build — for prospecting creators, building your own brand, and running outreach without burning accounts.

The website kit handles your authority site. This folder handles everything you do **after** your site is live: research the creators you want to partner with, build your own brand ecosystem, and send cold DMs without getting banned.

Three docs, each standalone:

---

## [`creator-research-template.md`](./creator-research-template.md)

Fill-in dossier you complete **before** pitching any creator. 13 sections covering: numbers, who they are, content patterns, audience demographics + psychographics, pain points, dream state, existing offers, monetisation gap, the core insight, recommended AI system, messaging rules, risks, and a one-page summary card.

**Time per dossier:** 60–120 minutes for a creator you don't know. The pitch lands when this work is done. Cold pitches that skip the dossier get ignored.

**When to use:** every time you're about to message a new creator. Fill out the template, save as `<creator-handle>-research.md`, then write the pitch from the dossier — never from a generic template.

---

## [`personal-brand-sop.md`](./personal-brand-sop.md)

Universal personal-brand framework. The 16-step actual buyer's journey, the 5 growth pillars (lead-gen, nurture, conversion, delivery, retention), the "friend first" principle, the guide-not-hero positioning, the 3 beliefs that need to hit 10/10 before a sale closes, the 4-part content ecosystem (Prove / Teach / Share / Give).

**When to use:** twice. Once when defining your **own** brand voice (so your site, content, and DMs feel consistent). Then again every time a client asks you to help them tighten their personal brand — the same framework applies to them.

---

## [`outreach-fundamentals.md`](./outreach-fundamentals.md)

Tool-agnostic playbook for cold DM outreach. Covers volume + pacing math (18–22 DMs/day, batched across 4 windows), the 4-week warmup ramp (5 → 10 → 15 → 20), the 8 send-safety rules (randomized spacing, profile engagement before DM, human typing, spintax, no links in DM #1, residential IP, work hours, session persistence), shadowban detection signals, opener template principles, reply funnel, lead-source schema, and the pre-launch checklist.

**When to use:** before you send a single cold DM. Read it once end-to-end. Reference it whenever you set up outreach for yourself or a client.

**The principles are tool-agnostic** — they work whether you DM manually, use Playwright/Puppeteer, an MCP server, or any other automation tool. The volume math, warmup ramp, and 8 safeguards apply universally.

## How these three connect

```
Your authority site (the kit)  ←  what creators see when you reach out
              ↓
  outreach-fundamentals.md     ←  HOW you safely message at volume
              ↓
  creator-research-template.md ←  WHO you message + WHAT you say
              ↓
  Reply / call / partnership
              ↓
  personal-brand-sop.md        ←  KEEPS the relationship + content compounding
```

The three together = operator workflow. The kit = the asset they need to look credible.

---

## What's NOT here (and why)

These are deliberately excluded to keep the playbook generic and impersonal:

- **Specific creator dossiers** — those are personal to whoever wrote them. The template is the reusable part.
- **Tool-specific scripts** (Playwright DM senders, Apify scrapers, Supabase configs) — these depend on credentials, accounts, and infrastructure the student doesn't have. Build your own per the principles.
- **Personal voice samples / brand-voice files** — every operator's voice is their own. The framework helps you find yours.
- **Specific lead-magnet / VSL / sales-page assets** — write your own using the copywriting frameworks in `design-reference/copywriting-frameworks.md`.

If you want any of those, build them yourself using the templates here as the spine.
