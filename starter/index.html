What if I'm not technical?

You don't need to be. I install the systems, train them on your voice, and hand you the controls. If you can use Instagram and Calendly, you can run this stack.

Do I need to be on social media already?

An audience helps but isn't required. The systems work for cold outreach, paid traffic, partnerships — anywhere leads come from. We adapt the playbook to where your demand actually is.

How is this different from other AI courses or coaches?

Courses teach you tools. Coaches give you advice. I install the operating system and run it with you. You get systems that work whether or not you watch a video.

What's the investment?

It depends on scope and what you already have in place. We map that on the audit call so the number reflects your actual business — not a generic package. No pitch, no pressure.
